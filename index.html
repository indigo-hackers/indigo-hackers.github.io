Indigo Hackers

This is a website for Indigo Hackers, a support group for people interested in IT/Computer Science and programming/coding and web design who happen to have one of the following: mental health conditions, medical conditions, LGBTIQ+, unemployed, socially isolated et etcera. Other people are welcome as allies as long as they are supportive. The name Indigo Hackers is inspired from the Blue Hackers Initiative - https://bluehackers.org/about It is not associated with the Blue Hackers Initiative or in any way directly with hacking. Our focus is to be a supportive environment where one can discuss our interest without any judgement re personal circumstances.. Our code of conduct is currently being worked on and will be posted soon.
